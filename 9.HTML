HTML Text Formating

  1. Bold (B)
    • Using bold text in a body paragraph helps emphasize a remark.
    • Bold can also differentiate headlines and titles from body paragraphs in the same font size.
  2. italic(i)
    • Italic Most commonly, italics are used for emphasis
    • Use italics to denote the titles of long creative works.
  3. Underline(U)
    • Underlines can be used to highlight important text.
    • Underlined text is used to help draw attention to the text.
